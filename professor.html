Summary

Iron Man, real name Tony Stark, is a genius billionaire, inventor, and philanthropist who dons a suit of armor to become a superhero. He is a founding member of the Avengers and plays a key role in saving the universe in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

Played byRobert Downey Jr. Appears inMarvel Cinematic Universe

Biography

After being kidnapped and gravely injured, Tony Stark builds a powered suit of armor to escape captivity. He later refines the suit and uses it to protect the world as Iron Man. His character arc is one of sacrifice, culminating in his selfless act to defeat Thanos in Avengers: Endgame (2019).
