Summary

Elsa is the Snow Queen of Arendelle, known for her magical ability to create and control ice and snow. She is a beloved Disney princess who learns to embrace her powers and herself in Frozen (2013) and Frozen II (2019).

Voiced byIdina Menzel Appears inFrozen, Frozen II

Biography

Born with magical ice powers, Elsa struggles to hide her abilities growing up, leading to isolation. After accidentally revealing her powers, she flees and ultimately learns to embrace her true self, bringing prosperity back to her kingdom and forging a stronger bond with her sister Anna.
